Return & Refund Policy

Thank you for shopping with us at {{ APP_NAME }}. We understand that there might be times when the products you receive don't quite match your expectations, and we're here to assist you in those instances. Please take a moment to carefully review this Return & Refund Policy, as it outlines your rights and responsibilities.

1. Returns

1.1. Eligibility for Returns:
- If you find yourself needing to request a return, you have 7 days from the date of product receipt to do so.
- To be eligible for a return, the product must be unused, in its original packaging, and in the same condition as when you received it.
- Please note that specific items like personalized goods or perishable items may not be eligible for return. This information will be clearly indicated on the product page.

1.2. Return Process:
- Should you wish to initiate a return, kindly reach out to our dedicated customer support team at [customer support email/phone number] within the specified return window.
- Provide your order number, details about the product, and the reason behind the return.
- Our friendly customer support team will guide you through the return process, which may also involve providing you with a return authorization if applicable.

1.3. Return Shipping:
- The responsibility for covering the cost of return shipping falls on you, except in cases where the return is due to a defective or incorrect product sent by us.

2. Refunds

2.1. Refund Eligibility:
- Once we've received and inspected the product you've returned, we'll promptly notify you regarding the approval or denial of your refund request.
- If your refund is approved, it will be processed within 5 days.
- Refunds will be granted exclusively for products that adhere to the eligibility criteria outlined in section 1.1.

2.2. Refund Process:
- Refunds will be issued using the initial payment method you used for your purchase.
- If, for any reason, the original payment method isn't available (e.g., an expired credit card), we'll arrange an alternative refund method in agreement with both parties.

2.3. Partial Refunds:
- On occasion, we may issue partial refunds for products returned with missing parts, packaging damage, or indications of use that significantly affect the product's value.

3. Exchanges

3.1. Exchanges:
- At present, we don't facilitate direct product exchanges. If you desire an exchange, you'll need to return the original item and then place a new order.

4. Damaged or Defective Products

4.1. Damaged or Defective Products:
- Should you receive a product damaged during shipping or with a defect, please notify us within 7 days of receiving the item.
- To expedite the resolution process, we may request photographic evidence of the damage or defect.

5. Contact Us

If you have any inquiries or concerns about our Return & Refund Policy, please don't hesitate to reach out to our dependable customer support team at [customer support email/phone number].

Please keep in mind that this policy is subject to change without prior notice. It's your responsibility to periodically review the policy for updates.
